Consumer identifiers redact to opaque tokens per agency policy. PHI never appears on the invoice — your AP team doesn't need it, and HIPAA prefers it gone.

Consolidation modes.

  • By month, by payer. Default — one invoice per payer per calendar month.
  • By department / cost center. Split a single payer into multiple invoices, GL-coded distinctly.
  • By project / case. For legal and ad-hoc engagements with a defined project code.
  • Per-event. Conferences, single-day events with their own PO.
  • Custom (Network tier). Combine any of the above with PO-matching rules.

Exports.

  • QuickBooks Online — direct OAuth export.
  • Xero — direct OAuth export.
  • NetSuite — SuiteApp connector, custom field mapping.
  • Bill.com — vendor-bill push.
  • Plain CSV / JSON — for everything else.
  • 1099-NEC and 1042-S issuance via track1099.
