For schedulers

The day-of view that doesn't make you swivel between five tabs.

Open jobs, claimed jobs, cancellations, replacements-needed — one screen. Keyboard-first. Conflict rules in plain English. Smart-fill that explains itself.

Watch the day-of demo Feature detail

One board, every job in flight.

Today's roster, today's jobs, today's exceptions — all in your viewport. Click a row to expand; press / to filter; press j and k to move through the queue. Multi-monitor: drag the day-of board to a second screen, keep job detail on the primary.

  • Filter by status, language, requestor, county, interpreter — combined.
  • Color-code by urgency (your rules — not ours).
  • Inline edit job details without opening a modal.
  • Bulk actions: send-offers, mark-confirmed, export-day.

Plain-English conflict rules

Every conflict rule has a one-sentence reason next to it, so you know why a warning fired — not just that it did.

  • No double-booking, ever. Hard block. Override requires explicit reason.
  • Back-to-back across counties. Soft warning with the drive-time estimate.
  • Skill mismatch. Warns when an interpreter's certs don't match what the venue requires.
  • Consumer preference miss. Surfaces "this requestor has booked Maria 4× this quarter" before you send the offer.
  • Team mismatch. Flags a CDI assignment with no voicer attached.
Smart-fill

Ranked candidates with a score breakdown — never a black box.

Hover any score and see the components: certification fit, location proximity, requestor preference, workload balance, prior performance with this consumer.

Transparent weights

You can see — and tune — every weight in the ranking. Default weights are public.

Cascade pattern

Parallel-3, first-claim-wins. Three top candidates get the offer simultaneously; whoever claims first locks the job.

Open marketplace

If cascade exhausts, the job opens to any qualified interpreter in your roster. First-claim, transparent log.

Keyboard shortcuts we got right.

If you live on the board, your hands stay on the keys.

Navigation

  • j / k — next / previous job
  • / — focus search
  • g then o — go to open jobs
  • ? — show all shortcuts

Actions

  • a — assign interpreter
  • r — request replacement
  • c — mark confirmed
  • x — cancel job (asks for reason)

Inspection

  • e — expand job detail
  • shift+e — expand all today
  • ? over a score — explain the ranking
  • cmd+k — command palette
