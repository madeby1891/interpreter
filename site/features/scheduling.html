Feature · Scheduling

Scheduling

Day-of board. Smart-fill with a score breakdown. Cascade pattern. Plain-English conflict rules.

The day-of board

Open jobs, claimed jobs, cancellations, replacements-needed — one screen. Filter by status, language, requestor, county, interpreter (combined). Color-code by urgency on your rules, not ours. Keyboard-first navigation: j/k///?. Drag the board to a second monitor.

Smart-fill with transparent ranking

Five-factor weighted score: certification fit, location proximity, requestor preference, workload balance, prior performance with this consumer. Every weight is visible and tunable. Hover any score and see the components. No black box.

Cascade pattern

Parallel-3, first-claim-wins by default. Three top candidates get the offer simultaneously; whoever claims first locks the job. Configurable per agency.

Conflict rules in plain English

Every rule has a one-sentence reason next to it. No double-booking, ever. Back-to-back across counties warns with the drive-time estimate. Skill mismatch flags missing certs. Consumer-preference miss surfaces prior bookings. CDI assignment without a voicer flags the gap.
